Chaffle

Description

A "chaffle," as it's become known, is low-carbohydrate alternative to bread needing only three ingredients. Ready in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup cheddar or Italian cheese, shredded
  • 2 tbsp almond flour

Directions

  1. Preheat the waffle maker.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the egg, almond flour, and cheese, until somewhat homogenous.
  3. Pour the batter into the waffle maker and cook until done.

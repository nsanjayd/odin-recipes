Chili

Description

Chili has no beans. A "chili" with beans deserves severe criticism, or worse.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 white onion, diced
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 tablespoons mild chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 6 ounces tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons cornmeal
  • 1 (28-ounce) can chunky tomato puree (or diced tomatoes)
  • 2 cups beef stock

Directions

  1. Brown the beef in a Dutch oven with the olive oil, for 5 or 6 minutes, until lightly browned.
  2. Add onions, green peppers, jalapeño, and garlic and cook them until they soften. Add cumin, pakrika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir.
  3. Add the tomato past the cornmeal. Stir in and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add the tomates and beef stock. Use the liquid to scrape off things stuck to the bottom.
  5. Bring it to a simmer and turn to low. Cook for at least ten minutes, or hours. Check periodically. If the chili looks dry, add water.
